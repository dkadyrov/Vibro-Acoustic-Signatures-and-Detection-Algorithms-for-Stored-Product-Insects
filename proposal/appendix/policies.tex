\chapter{Policy and Regulatory Considerations}

The research and products developed as part of the stored product insect detection study were funded by the Cross-Border Threat Screening and Supply Chain Defense (CBTS) under grant 18STCBT00001. This office no longer exists and the publications and work no longer need to be reviewed and approved.

Daniel Kadyrov is currently a full-time employee of the National Urban Security Technology Laboratory (NUSTL), within the Science and Technology Directorate, a part of the Department of Homeland Security (DHS). Publications and work products need to be reviewed and approved by the DHS Science and Technology ethics office and the NUSTL leadership prior to public release.
