    \texttt{5\_5\_spectra\_comparison.py}         & Generates Figure \ref{fig:spectra_insect_noise}                                                              \\
    \texttt{5\_6\_nspa\_optimization.py}          & Generates Figure \ref{fig:nspa_optimization}                                                                 \\
    \texttt{5\_7\_waveform\_comparison\_newhz.py} & Generates Figure \ref{fig:waveforms 1565-6000}                                                               \\
    \texttt{5\_8\_nspa\_metric\_updated.py}       & Generates Figure \ref{fig:nspa_distribution}                                                                 \\
